We cannot and do not guarantee specific results from any dental treatment or procedure. Individual results vary based on numerous factors beyond our control.

  • Patients shown may have had multiple procedures

Estimates of treatment duration and longevity of dental work are approximations based on typical outcomes. Actual duration and longevity may be shorter or longer depending on individual circumstances and maintenance.

Specific risks will be discussed during your consultation and documented in informed consent forms.

8. Emergency Situations

Dental Emergencies

This website is NOT intended for emergency situations. If you are experiencing a dental emergency, contact us immediately at 09994827342 or visit the nearest hospital emergency room.

8.1 When to Seek Emergency Care

Seek immediate emergency care if you experience:

  • Severe, uncontrolled bleeding
  • Facial swelling affecting breathing or swallowing
  • Trauma resulting in broken jaw or severe facial injury
  • Knocked-out permanent teeth (seek care within 30 minutes)
  • Signs of serious infection (high fever, severe pain, rapid swelling)
  • Allergic reactions to dental treatment or medications

8.2 After-Hours Emergencies

For after-hours dental emergencies:

  • Call: 09994827342 (our emergency line)
  • Leave a message with your name, contact number, and nature of emergency
  • For life-threatening emergencies, call local emergency services immediately

8.3 Email & Website Forms Not for Emergencies

Do NOT use email (drjoyangel@gmail.com) or website contact forms for emergency situations. These are not monitored 24/7 and may not receive immediate response.
